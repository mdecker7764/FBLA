Best Ways To cook Food

  • Chicken
    1. Brine: fill a bowl with lukewarm water, not hot, and pour in a handful of salt and mix it until its mostly disolved. then place the chicken in the water for around fifteen minutes.
    2. Brush: Brush the chicken breasts liberally on both sides with melted butter to prevent it sticking to the pan.
    3. Rub: To keep in simple, just sprinkle some salt and pepper on the chicken.
    4. Roast: Set oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit and set a timer to 15-18 minutes so that the chicken gets cooked properly
    5. Rest: No matter what let your chicken rest before cutting them, if you cut into the chicken right away you'll loose all the juice from it, letting it rest makes the chicken extremely tender and delicious.
